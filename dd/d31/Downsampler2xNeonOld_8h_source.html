3 Downsampler2xNeonOld.h
4 Author: Laurent de Soras, 2016
5
6Downsamples by a factor 2 the input signal, using NEON instruction set.
7
8This object must be aligned on a 16-byte boundary!
9
10The output is delayed from 1 sample, compared to the theoretical formula (or
11FPU implementation).
12
13Template parameters:
14 - NC: number of coefficients, > 0
