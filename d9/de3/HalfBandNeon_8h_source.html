3 HalfBandNeon.h
4 Author: Laurent de Soras, 2016
5
6Half-band filter, low-pass or high-pass, using NEON instruction set.
7
8This object must be aligned on a 16-byte boundary!
9
10The output is delayed from 2 sample, compared to the theoretical formula (or
11FPU implementation).
12
13Template parameters:
14 - NC: number of coefficients, > 0
