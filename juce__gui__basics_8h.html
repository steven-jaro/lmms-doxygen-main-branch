◆ JUCE_USE_XINERAMA

#define JUCE_USE_XINERAMA   1

Config: JUCE_USE_XINERAMA Enables Xinerama multi-monitor support (Linux only). Unless you specifically want to disable this, it's best to leave this option turned on. This will be used as a fallback if JUCE_USE_XRANDR not set or libxrandr cannot be found. Note that your users do not need to have Xinerama installed for your JUCE app to run, as the availability of Xinerama is queried during runtime.
