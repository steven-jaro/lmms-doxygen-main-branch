enum Presonus::ContextInfo::ChannelIndexMode

Channel index mode.

Enumerator
kFlatIndex 

channel indices are contiguous (example: track 1, track 2, bus 3, bus 4)

kPerTypeIndex 

channel indices restarts at zero for each type (example: track 1, track 2, bus 1, bus 2)
