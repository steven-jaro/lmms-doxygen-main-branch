float juce::PenDetails::rotation

The rotation of the pen device in radians. Indicates the clockwise rotation, or twist, of the pen. The default is 0.

◆ tiltX

float juce::PenDetails::tiltX

Indicates the angle of tilt of the pointer in a range of -1.0 to 1.0 along the x-axis where a positive value indicates a tilt to the right. The default is 0.

◆ tiltY

float juce::PenDetails::tiltY

Indicates the angle of tilt of the pointer in a range of -1.0 to 1.0 along the y-axis where a positive value indicates a tilt toward the user. The default is 0.
