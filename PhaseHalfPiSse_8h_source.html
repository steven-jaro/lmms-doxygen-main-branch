3 PhaseHalfPiSse.h
4 Author: Laurent de Soras, 2005
5
6From the input signal, generates two signals with a pi/2 phase shift, using
7SSE instruction set.
8
9This object must be aligned on a 16-byte boundary!
10
11The output is delayed from 2 sample, compared to the theoretical formula (or
12FPU implementation).
13
14Template parameters:
15 - NC: number of coefficients, > 0
